Asphalt is Low Maintenance

Asphalt driveways are low maintenance. They do not require regular cleaning or sealing, and they will last for many years with minimal care. Asphalt driveways are also very easy to repair if they crack or develop potholes.

Asphalt is Attractive

Asphalt driveways can be stained or dyed to create various looks. This makes them an attractive option for many homeowners. However, asphalt driveways are also susceptible to staining and fading over time. This can be caused by various factors, including exposure to the sun, weathering, and environmental pollutants. If your asphalt driveway is beginning to look stained or faded, there are a few things you can do to restore its original look.

One option is to power wash the driveway with a high-pressure washer. This will remove any dirt, grime, or other debris built upon the driveway’s surface. It is essential to be careful when power washing an asphalt driveway, as too much pressure can damage the surface. Another option is to use a commercial cleaner specifically designed for cleaning asphalt driveways. These cleaners are available at most hardware stores and home improvement centers.

Once the driveway is clean, you may also want to apply a sealer. This will help protect the surface of the asphalt from staining and fading in the future. There are a variety of sealers available, so be sure to choose one that is compatible with your type of asphalt driveway. Follow the instructions on the packaging carefully to ensure proper application.
